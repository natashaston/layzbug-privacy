Privacy Policy — Layzbug

Last updated: May 2026

Layzbug ("the app") is a personal walking tracker for Android developed by Natash Aston Cutinha. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Physical activity data — daily step counts and walk durations, read from Android Health Connect with your explicit permission.
  • Account information — your name and email address, collected via Google Sign-In for the purpose of saving your data to your personal account.
  • Walk records — dates, durations, and distances of your walks, stored in our database to power your history and statistics.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: tracking your walks, displaying your history, and calculating your statistics. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the app itself.

3. Data Storage

Walk records and account data are stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Data is associated with your Google account ID and is accessible only to you.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — used for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Android Health Connect — used to read step and activity data from your device. No health data is shared with third parties.
  • Supabase — used for data storage. Governed by Supabase's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties beyond the services listed above, which are necessary to operate the app.

6. Health Connect Permissions

Layzbug requests access to step count and activity data through Android Health Connect. This permission is used exclusively to detect and log your walks. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device's Health Connect settings.

7. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will permanently delete your account and all associated walk records within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Layzbug is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: natashaston@yahoo.com
